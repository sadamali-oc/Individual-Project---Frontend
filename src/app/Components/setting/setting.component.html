Dashboard Guidance
  • Manage all events, users, and system settings.
  • View and generate detailed reports.
  • Approve or reject organizer and user requests.
  • Create and manage events for your club or department.
  • View attendees for your events and manage registrations.
  • Update event details and communicate with participants.
  • View upcoming events and register for them.
  • Check your registered events and participation status.
  • Update your personal profile and contact details.
